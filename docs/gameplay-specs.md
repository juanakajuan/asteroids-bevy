- Top-down 2D space shooter with wraparound screen edges

- Player controls a rotating spaceship in open space

- Thrust-based movement with inertia (ship keeps drifting)

- Ability to rotate left/right and accelerate forward

- Shoot bullets/projectiles to destroy asteroids

- Large asteroids split into smaller faster pieces when hit

- Flying saucers/UFO enemies occasionally appear and shoot at the player

- Collision with asteroids, enemy shots, or UFOs destroys the ship

- Limited lives with score-based extra lives

- Increasing difficulty as waves progress

- Simple vector-style graphics and arcade sound effects

- Hyperspace/emergency teleport feature with risk of reappearing in danger

- High-score chasing as the core long-term objective
